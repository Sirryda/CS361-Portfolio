Pet of the week!


Heyo!! My name is Daisy and I am a 2 year old female Bully mix. I am a friendly pup with soft fur and spots like a cow. I love giving kisses and laying in cozy beds. I also have a lot of energy, and will need positive outlets to keep me mentally and physically stimulated. When I'm out for a walk, I tend to be neutral towards other dogs passing by. I have met a few different dogs here at the shelter, but was not so sure how I felt about them. I think at this time, being the only doggie in the home would be the best fit for me. I will always be ready to snuggle and watch movies with you, but also always be ready to go for a long walk. If you give me some love and attention, I'll give you lots of laid-back friendship in return! Please come meet me today :)
